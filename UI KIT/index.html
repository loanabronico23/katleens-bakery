    KATLEEN'S BAKERY

    OUR STORY

    Katleen, the fresh face of Marseille's Panier district, is a pastry chef full of determination and sweetness. From an early age, she was fascinated by the art of pastry-making, secretly dreaming of creating sweet delights that would warm the hearts of her friends and neighbors. After years in the office world, she finally decided to follow her heart and make her passion her profession.

    Today, her boutique is brimming with deliciously concocted cakes, colorful macaroons and pastries that delight the taste buds. She brings a sweet touch to the lives of the inhabitants of Le Panier, transforming every day into an occasion to celebrate gourmandise.

    Thanks to the success of her boutique, Katleen soon realized that the basket outlet alone would not be enough to satisfy the demands of the whole town. So she came up with an innovative solution: to open 5 dark kitchens strategically located around the city - la joliette, mazargues, la valentine, le prado and la rose. This latest idea now enables her to offer delivery of her sweets throughout the city.
